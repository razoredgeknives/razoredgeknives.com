General Pricing

Razor Edge Knives LLC provides the upstate of South Carolina with a professional, local, blade sharpening service that will make your blades better than new! Our nation-wide mail-in knife sharpening service is also available if you live elsewhere in the States.

Prices for Services

  • Custom Knives – Start at $30 and goes up depending on options. This price includes an extra $20 fee for the extreme care given to your custom knife (taping off the blade, handling gingerly, symmetrical bevels, etc) unless you specify otherwise. Also, no power equipment will touch your Custom unless agreed upon. Custom sharpening (w/ no powered equipment) is limited to blades 4″ and under. Here is our tiered pricing structure:
    • Up to $2k in value: $20
    • $2k-4k in value: $40
    • $4k-8k in value: $60
    • $8k+ custom quoted
  • Knife Regrinds – start at $50 and go up. This is for a basic 60 grit finish. Wharncliffs, Tantos, blade conversions, or higher grit finishing adds additional to price. This is custom priced per knife, however, general prices are: $50 for blades up to 5″, $60 for blades 5″-8″, and $70 for blades 8″-12″. I don’t really regrind any blades over this length unless worked out prior. Upgrades over base price:
    • 220 grit satin finish: Add $25
    • 600 grit premium satin finish: Add $60
    • Tanto regrinds: Add $10
    • Zero grinds (taken up to 220 grit finish): Add $30
    • Grind conversions (hollow to flat, flat to convex, conventional to tanto, etc): Add $5-15 depending on how complicated
    • Blade sterilization/logo delete on flats (satin finished/hand rubbed): Add $40. If you want logos deleted on grind then you will have to do this in conjunction w/ a regrind.
  • Plain-edged, single-bladed knives – $6/knife regardless of size. Tanto blade add $1. These are done on a slow speed, water cooled belt sander and many of my customers fit in this category.
  • Hand Sharpening of knives (i.e. Done on the Wicked Edge Precision Sharpener) starts at $11 and goes up depending on the desired options. Mirrored edges are done at my discretion and cost between $35 and $50 and will be able to whittle a hair on most knives. Notes:
    • All knives over 4″ in blade length must be reprofiled on the belt sander first prior to finishing on the Wicked Edge.
    • If any reprofile work is done on blades over 4″ blade length an additional $2-3 may be charged for the extra time/work.
    • If 4″ or under then I can either match the angle for the listed price or reprofile by hand for an extra fee (specify if you want this done in the mail in form by stating "no machine reprofile").
    • Default method will be to set edge angle at 15 dps or as low as feasible (determined by the knife grind and thickness) by belt sander and then micro bevel on the Wicked Edge at about 20 dps as this is a very durable edge yet will cut exceptionally well.
  • Double-edged knives/daggers – cost double whatever type blade/options you have (i.e. straight edge, serrated, etc). Please note these will be sharpened on machine only – no Wicked Edge sharpening option available unless done in conjunction w/ a regrind.
  • Japanese Sushi Knives – $15/ea (done on diamond or waterstones by hand), more if repair needed. Secondary bevel will be applied or matched.
  • Multi-bladed, pocket knives – $4/blade
  • Serrated knives < 6″ blade length – $8/blade. (including full or partially serrated).
  • Serrated knives > 6″ blade length – $11/blade. This will mainly apply to kitchen bread knives. Note: we also offer a Christmas Tree shearing knife sharpening service (which are serrated blades) and this costs in the range of $1-2/inch depending on how damaged they are.
  • Straight Razors – $30/blade (note: please do not send in poor quality razors as they will not be honed), these are honed to the sub-micron level for a superior shave! Any damage (rust, nicks, etc) that needs to be repaired will be $10 extra – this is verified and checked under microscopic examination of your edge and continued throughout the honing progression for the finest edge and shaving experience!
  • Machetes – $10/blade.
  • Swords – $1/inch sharpened unless complete reprofiling is needed, then it could be $2/inch.
  • Hatchet – $8/blade unless extremely damaged, if so call for a quote.
  • Axe – $12, Double-edged Axe – $20
  • Scissors (for general household/industrial use) – $8 ea (depending on condition of the scissors bulk discounts apply in quantities of >20 scissors (see the “Specials” above). Note: we can do industrial scissor sharpening in practically any quantity).
  • Surgical Shears & Surgical Scissors (typically for veterinary purposes) – $8/ each
  • Lawn Mower Blades – $10/blade. You remove and bring to us.We will sharpen and balance the blade.
  • Garden tools – Call for a price
  • Chisels – $5/chisel and this will have 220 grit working edge

* If you have something you want sharpened and do not see it please ask./p>

  • To grind down the "return"/bolster (on a kitchen knife) the cost is $3/knife.
  • Completely Wasted Serrations – If we have to completely regrind and profile the serrations back into your blade then there will be an additional fee of $8/blade.
