Pricing

Blade Sharpening/Repair

  • Plain-edged, single-bladed knives - $6/knife regardless of size. Tanto blade add $1.
  • Serrated knives < 6" blade length - $8/blade. (including full or partially serrated).
  • Serrated knives > 6" blade length - $11/blade. This will mainly apply to kitchen bread knives.
  • Multi-bladed, pocket knives - $4/blade
  • Scissors (for general household/industrial use) - $8 ea
  • Double-edged knives/daggers - cost double whatever type blade/options you have (i.e. straight edge, serrated, etc). Please note these will be sharpened on machine only and we only sharpen daggers limited to $50 in value.
  • Machetes - $1/inch sharpened unless complete reprofiling is needed, then it could be $2/inch.
  • Swords - $1/inch sharpened unless complete reprofiling is needed, then it could be $2/inch.
  • Hatchet - $10/blade unless extremely damaged, if so call for a quote.
  • Axe - $12, Double-edged Axe - $20
  • Surgical Shears & Surgical Scissors (typically for veterinary purposes) - $8/ each
  • Chisels - $5/chisel and this will have 220 grit working edge.
  • Garden tools - Call for a price
  • Lawn Mower Blades - we do not sharpen these. Sorry!

Wicked Edge Sharpening Service

Hand Sharpening of knives (i.e. Done on the Wicked Edge Precision Sharpener) – inch lengths are rounded to the nearest whole number.

  • If 4″ or under – $4/inch. Price includes either matching the existing angle or a free reprofile by belt sander then finished w/ a micro bevel on the WEPS. We can also reprofile by hand for an additional fee of $6/inch (specify if you want this done in the mail in form).
  • If over 4″ – also $4/inch, however, the only way we sharpen these on the Wicked Edge is if the edge is first taken to the belt sander (water cooled, slow speed) and the edge angle either matched or reprofiled (default is to reprofile somewhat). The additional cost includes an edge reprofile which will help with cutting ability greatly. We then add a micro-bevel with the Wicked Edge to get the nice crisp apex and some strength to resist edge deformation.
  • Reprofile work by hand only (no belt sander) – add $6/inch to the above fee.
  • Advanced Sharpening/Mirrored edges are done at my discretion and are $11/inch. These will be able to whittle a hair on most knives. Tanto mirrored edges will be $10 extra.
  • Tanto edges w/ regular sharpening are $4 extra.
  • Custom Knives – Starts at $15/inch and goes up depending on options. Click here for more info. Also, no power equipment will touch your Custom unless agreed upon. Custom sharpening (w/ no powered equipment) is limited to blades 4″ and under. Tanto’s are +$10.

Knife Regrinds

Knife Regrinds start at $60 and go up. This is for a basic 60 grit finish and includes sharpening on the Wicked Edge afterwards. Various blade shapes and options add additional costs. Basic prices are $60 for blades up to 5″, $70 for blades 5″-8″, and $85 for blades 8″-12″. I don’t really regrind any blades over this length unless worked out prior. Upgrades over base price:

  • 220 grit satin finish: Add $25
  • 600 grit premium satin finish: Add $60
  • Zero grinds (taken up to 400 grit finish): Add $35
  • Hollow grinds: Add $10
  • Tanto regrinds: Add $10
  • Wharncliffe blade shape: Add $10
  • Grind conversions (hollow to flat, flat to convex, conventional to tanto, etc): Add $5-15 depending on how complicated
  • Blade sterilization/logo delete on flats (satin finished/hand rubbed): Add $40. If you want logos deleted on grind then you will have to do this in conjunction w/ a regrind.
