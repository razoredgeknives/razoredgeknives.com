About Razor Edge Knives

Razor Edge Knives LLC is operated by its owner. Josh has been sharpening knives since the beginning of 2010 in the upstate area of South Carolina. He has served in both the military and law enforcement and knows the value and importance of a sharp knife and blade in every circumstance.

What Makes Us Different?

First and foremost we are a Christian company that only conducts business to the glory of the Lord! We are honest and care about each and every customer! We guarantee that you will be completely satisfied with your experience using us to sharpen your knives or your money back!

Most sharpening companies grind knives at high speed and dry - we do the opposite. We use slow speeds, sharp abrasives, and liquid cooling during the grinding process.

We let our work and customers speak for us. See our testimonials page for reviews from our satisfied customers!
We are constantly expanding and use various approaches to sharpen and re-craft your tools. We do not limit ourselves to one approach, and this is key!
